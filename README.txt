# Empire Attacks: Tower Defense

A brower-based tower defense game built with HTML and JavaScript

"In a world where evil remains rampant, the king orders a final attack to reclaim the corrupt land and bring glory to the empire."

## Game Features

- Wave-based gameplay: Survive 6 increasingly difficult rounds, culminating in a final wave
- Tower Defense mechanics: Place defense towers on designated tiles
- Resource management: Earn coins by defeating enemies and spend them on new towers
- Health system: Start the game off with 10 hearts, lose one for each enemy that reaches the end
- Progessive difficulty: Each wave spawns more enemies than the last

## How to Play
Combine two adjacent Level 3 towers (200 coins) to create unique Fusion Towers:

- Earthquake Titan (Rock + Rock): Freezes all enemies periodically
- Orbital Cannon (Sniper + Sniper): Infinite range with area damage ultimate
- Minigun Fortress (Rapid + Rapid): Multi-target attacks with poison ultimate
- Railgun Destroyer (Rock + Sniper): Piercing shots that push enemies back
- Grenade Launcher (Rock + Rapid): Explosive splash damage
- Smart Turret (Sniper + Rapid): Intelligent targeting with slow effect

Maximum 3 fusions per game!

1. Start the Game: 
    - Click "START GAME" button on the tittle screen
2. Place Towers:
    - Select tower type from the menu at the bottom
    - Click on the white highlighted tiles to place towers (each cost 50 coins)
    - Towers automatically target and shoot enemies in range
3. Upgrade Towers:
    - Click on a placed tower to upgrade it (costs vary by level)
    - Level 1 → 2: 75 coins
    - Level 2 → 3: 100 coins
4. Merge Towers:
    - Upgrade two adjacent towers to Level 3
    - Click on one tower when merge option appears (200 coins)
    - Click on the adjacent Level 3 tower to complete fusion

5. Defend Your Base:
    - Prevent enemies from reachinf the right side of the screen
    - Each enemy that escapes costs you 1 heart
6. Earn Resources:
    - Each defeated enemy awards 19 coins
    - Use coins to build more towers
7. Win Condition:
    - Survive all 4 waves without losing all of your hearts
8. Lose Condition:
    - Reach 0 hearts

## Controls

 - Mouse Movement: Highlight available tower placement tiles and view tower info
 - Left Click: Place tower on highlighted tile or upgrade/merge existing towers (only if you have enough coins)
 - Shift + Click: Sell tower for 50% refund
 - ESC: Cancel fusion mode

## Tower Stats by level

### Rock Launcher
- Level 1: 250 range, 20 damage, moderate fire rate
- Level 2: 300 range, 30 damage, faster fire rate
- Level 3: 350 range, 300 damage, fast fire rate + Chain Lightning (every 5th shot)

### Sniper Tower
- Level 1: 400 range, 50 damage, slow fire rate
- Level 2: 450 range, 75 damage, moderate fire rate
- Level 3: 500 range, 750 damage, moderate fire rate + Piercing Shot

### Rapid Fire
- Level 1: 180 range, 8 damage, very fast fire rate
- Level 2: 220 range, 12 damage, very fast fire rate
- Level 3: 260 range, 120 damage, very fast fire rate + Bullet Storm ultimate

## Fusion Tower Stats

### Earthquake Titan (Rock + Rock)
- Range: 450 | Damage: 10,000 | Fire Rate: 0.2
- Ultimate: Freeze all enemies for 5 seconds (cooldown: 10s)

### Orbital Cannon (Sniper + Sniper)
